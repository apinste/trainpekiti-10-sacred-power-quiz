About This Quiz

This quiz helps you understand which inner power is most active in your life right now. These powers are not personality types — they are states that rise and fall depending on your energy, stress load, relationships, and life season.

Your results will show:

  • Primary Power — what’s strongest right now
  • Emerging Power — what’s developing
  • Shadow Power — what needs attention

You can revisit this quiz anytime you feel a shift — monthly, seasonally, or during times of transition.

Select Your Interpretation Path

Choose the path that best matches your energetic orientation. The questions are the same; the interpretation changes.

Feminine Path: cycles, intuition, emotional depth, embodiment, relational wisdom.
Masculine Path: warriorship, tactical awareness, leadership, discipline, strategic clarity.

Please select a path before taking the quiz.
